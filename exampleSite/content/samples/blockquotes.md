## Simple Blockquote

> The best way to predict the future is to invent it.

## Multi-paragraph Blockquote

> This is the first paragraph of the blockquote.
>
> This is the second paragraph. Blockquotes can contain multiple paragraphs and other markdown elements.

## Blockquote with Attribution

Use markdown attributes to add author attribution:

> Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic.
{author="Arthur C. Clarke"}

> The only way to do great work is to love what you do.
{author="Steve Jobs"}

## Blockquote with Formatting

> You can include **bold**, *italic*, and `code` in blockquotes.
>
