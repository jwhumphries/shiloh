## Unordered Lists

- First item
- Second item
  - Nested item
  - Another nested item
    - Deeply nested
- Third item

Alternative markers:

* Asterisk item
* Another asterisk item

+ Plus item
+ Another plus item

## Ordered Lists

1. First step
2. Second step
3. Third step
   1. Sub-step one
   2. Sub-step two
4. Fourth step

## Mixed Lists

1. First ordered item
   - Unordered sub-item
   - Another sub-item
2. Second ordered item
   1. Ordered sub-item
   2. Another ordered sub-item

## Task Lists

- [x] Completed task
- [x] Another completed task
- [ ] Incomplete task
- [ ] Another incomplete task

## Definition Lists

Term 1
: Definition for term 1

Term 2
: Definition for term 2
: Additional definition
