Explore advanced formatting techniques available in Hugo and the Shiloh theme.

## Custom Styling

While the theme provides beautiful defaults, you can customize as needed.

### Typography Enhancements

Use **bold**, *italic*, ***both***, and ~~strikethrough~~ effectively.

### Special Characters

- Em dash: —
- En dash: –
- Ellipsis: …
- Quotes: "smart quotes" and 'single quotes'

## Mathematical Expressions

For technical content, you might need mathematical notation (requires KaTeX):

```
E = mc²
```
